Arrow OS

Official Maintainer for the ASUS MAX M2.

XDA Thread

ArrowOS is an AOSP based project started with the aim of keeping things simple, clean and neat. We added just the right and mostly used stuff that will be actually USEFUL at the end of the day, aiming to deliver smooth performance with better battery life.

PBRP

Official Maintainer for the ASUS MAX M2.

PitchBlack Recovery Project is Open Source organization founded on 28th March 2018 collectively by the developers from different countries with an aim to provide the world’s most advanced recovery with better customization’s, themes and features.
